Product Design Leader · UX & AI

Two decades leading UX research, design teams, and product strategy — with a steady focus on AI experiences that stay clear, humane, and trustworthy.

See my work → About me

Clarity, trust & shared ownership

Inclusive by default. We write things down, invite quieter voices early, and rotate facilitation. Context is shared, ownership is clear.

Evidence-led product strategy

I start with people and outcomes. Research frames the problem and shapes measurable goals, then shows up in what actually ships.

Systems-level impact

I apply systems thinking to patterns, naming, and guidance that scale across products. Fewer one-offs, fewer debates — the system does the heavy lifting.

AI with intention

Start with real use cases and risks. Design for usefulness, respect, and transparency. Then keep a steady loop: review, learn, evolve.

Experience design in an AI world

Start with why, then earn trust.

A deliberate process that puts human agency first: clarify goals, choose the right AI moments, design for transparency and safety — then ship, observe, and evolve.
